Engineering

Creating something new is in my blood

How did we get here?

My mother was an engineer in the 80s. The discrimination she faced turned me away from the discipline all together at a young age, but in high school, I wound up leading the CAD for my robotics team. I loved every second of it; things just made sense. I continued my pursuit of engineering at Pitt as a mechanical engineering major, because I believe that engineering has the potential for great improvement to the world. Hunger could see an end through hydroponics, homelessness through well-designed housing projects, and scarcity through intelligent reuse and repurposing of materials.
