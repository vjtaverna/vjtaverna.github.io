Education

A talented maker is worthless without a way to communicate what they know

Why education?

Education and communication touch everything we do. Every single thing that we do in this world—every job, hobby, talent, interest—had to be learned. Mastery over education means effective communication with the world and with oneself; practicing these skills is my way of keeping a sharp mind. It's been said thousands of times that you don't truly understand something unless you can explain it to a child, so that's what I'm doing—with everything.

Pitt Makerspace Workshops

Over the course of three weeks, students from all disciplines were guided through the design, manufacture, and coding of a fully programmable 12 button macropad. The machine works on a RP2040 running CircuitPy HID libraries.

Curriculum Projects

In my work at Fluxspace, I develop the tools for teachers to educate students. Recently, we've been made aware that elementary teachers do not know how to handle the pivot away from the old state education standards and towards the new STEELS standards. I began the development of STEELS aligned curriculum to promote this learning among students.

  • Steels me!

Frontier in Education Presentation

For roughly a year, I engaged in research regarding student learning in a flipped classroom format. I wrote MATLAB scripts to parse data and identify student behaviors to group them into bins for statistical analysis such as ANOVA. This culminated in a trip to Texas for IEEE's Frontiers in Education Conference to present our findings.

  • I love research!
